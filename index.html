How it works

Gesture-first,
zero friction

Every interaction is a swipe or a pull. No menus, no buttons in the way.

↓

Pull to add

Pull the list down to reveal an inline text field. Type your task and let go.

→

Swipe right to complete

A satisfying swipe marks the task as done. It flies out of the list.

←

Swipe left to delete

Delete with a swipe. A 4-second undo toast catches any mistakes.

⠿

Drag to reorder

Grab the handle on the right side of any task and drag it where it belongs.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Dimmly combines tasks and habits in one place without the complexity.

Daily repeats

Mark any task to repeat daily. After completion it reappears the next day automatically. Alarms carry forward too.

Habit streak tracking

Set how many times per day you want to complete a task — up to 7. Dots on the card fill in as you go. Resets every day.

Alarms

Quick options (1h, 3h, tomorrow) or a full date picker. Local notifications — no server, no account, no data sent anywhere.

25 color palette themes

Each list gets its own palette. Named after cities — Nápoles, Atenas, Estambul. Color-coded navigation between lists.

Home screen widgets

See your tasks without opening the app. Supports all widget sizes and list modes.

CSV import & export

Bring your tasks in or take them out. Plain CSV format — first line is the list name, the rest are task titles.

List modes

Three views: Normal (list), Ranked (numbered), and Top (prioritized). Switch per list depending on how you think.

No account required

Open the app and start adding tasks. No sign-up, no email, no onboarding tour. Your data stays on your device.
